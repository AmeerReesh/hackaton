Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein is a Jewish inventor who revolutionized science with his theories of relativity, which reshaped our understanding of time, space and energy. His work laid the foundation for modern physics and had a profound impact on the fields.

Ismail al-Jazari

Ismail al-Jazari, a Muslim inventor from the 12th century is known as the "father of robotics" due to his groundbreaking work in the field of automata, which are self-operating machines.

About us

who we are?

We are a team of five developers, representing both Israeli and Arab backgrounds, united by a shared vision. Our goal is to highlight the profound contributions from both cultures, celebrating the achievements of inventors and innovators who have shaped the world.

Our goal?

Our mission is to foster cultural understanding through a unique, engaging game experience. This solitaire game showcases inventors from both Jewish and Arab communities, encouraging players to explore and appreciate the inventive minds that stem from these rich heritages.
