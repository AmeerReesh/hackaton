Inventions Rules

Objective

The goal of Inventions is to move all cards to the four foundation piles, sorted by suit and in ascending order from 1-10 with tears.

Setup

Cards are dealt into seven columns, with each subsequent column having one more card than the previous. Only the top card in each column is face-up.

Gameplay

Move cards between columns by building sequences in descending order and alternating colors. Only Kings can fill empty columns.

Winning

Win by sorting all cards into foundation piles by suit from 1-10 .
